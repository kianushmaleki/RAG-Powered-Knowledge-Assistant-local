Aura Assurance follows a Pro-Rata Refund Model for all policy cancellations. Customers may cancel their policy at any time for any reason, but the method of cancellation determines the final settlement of the "Unearned Premium."

Cancellation Methods:

Standard Cancellation: Requires a 10-day written notice sent via the "Secure Message Center" in the Aura Portal.

Proof of Sale: If you have sold your home or vehicle, provide a "Closing Disclosure" or "Bill of Sale" dated within 30 days to receive a full pro-rata refund back to the date of the sale.

Flat Cancellation: Only available within the first 48 hours of a new policy's effective date, provided no claims have been filed. This results in a 100% refund, minus a $25 "Underwriting Fee."

Short-Rate Penalty:
If a policy is cancelled by the policyholder mid-term without proof of replacement coverage or asset sale, Aura Assurance reserves the right to apply a "Short-Rate" penalty of 10% of the remaining unearned premium. This covers the administrative costs associated with the early termination of the contract.
