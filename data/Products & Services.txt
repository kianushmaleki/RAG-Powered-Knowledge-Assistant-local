The Aura Assurance Shield Home Plan (SHP-2026) is our flagship residential property insurance product, designed for primary residences, secondary vacation homes, and high-value urban condos. Launched on January 1, 2024, the SHP-2026 replaces the legacy "HomeGuard" series. This policy provides "all-risk" coverage, meaning all perils are covered unless specifically excluded in Section 4.

Key coverage areas include:

Dwelling (Coverage A): Protects the physical structure. The minimum valuation for this plan is $250,000, with an automatic "Inflation Guard" adjustment applied every six months on June 1 and December 1.

Other Structures (Coverage B): Covers detached garages, fences, and tool sheds up to 10% of the Dwelling limit.

Personal Property (Coverage C): Protects belongings at 50% of the Dwelling limit. Note that high-value items like jewelry or fine art are capped at $2,500 unless an "Aura Luxury Rider" is added.

Loss of Use (Coverage D): Provides up to 24 months of living expenses if the home is uninhabitable due to a covered claim.

The Shield Home Plan is currently available in 42 states, excluding Florida, Louisiana, and coastal areas of Texas.
