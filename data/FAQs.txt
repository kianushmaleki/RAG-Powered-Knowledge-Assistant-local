The following are the most common inquiries regarding Aura Assurance policies as of the Q1 2026 update.

When does my coverage actually begin?
Coverage starts at 12:01 AM local time on the "Effective Date" listed on your Declarations Page. We do not offer backdated coverage under any circumstances.

Can I add a roommate or a domestic partner to my policy?
Yes. Under the "Additional Insured" provision, you may add one non-relative resident for a flat $15 annual administrative fee, provided they have a clean CLUE (Comprehensive Loss Underwriting Exchange) report for the last 3 years.

What is an "Escrow Payment," and why is my bank paying my bill?
If you have a mortgage, your lender likely pays your insurance from an escrow account. Aura sends the "Annual Premium Notice" directly to your lender 45 days before the renewal date. If you receive a "Notice of Non-Payment," contact your mortgage servicer’s "Insurance Disbursements" department immediately.

Does my policy cover mold or fungus?
Standard policies include a $5,000 "Fungi, Wet or Dry Rot, or Bacteria" limit. This is only triggered if the mold is a direct result of a covered water peril (e.g., a burst pipe). It does not cover mold caused by high humidity or lack of maintenance.
