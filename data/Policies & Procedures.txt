Filing a claim with Aura Assurance must follow the Standardized Claims Protocol (SCP-2026) to ensure timely reimbursement. All claims must be initiated within 60 days of the "Date of Incident" to avoid a mandatory 15% "Late-Filing Deductible" penalty.

The Five-Step Process:

Immediate Notification: Report the loss via the Aura Mobile App or by calling the 24/7 Claims Hotline at 1-800-AURA-NOW. You will receive a unique Claim Reference Number (CRN) immediately.

Mitigation: Policyholders are contractually obligated to prevent further damage (e.g., tarping a roof or turning off water mains). Aura will reimburse up to $1,500 in emergency mitigation costs without prior adjuster approval.

Documentation: Upload at least 5 high-resolution photos of the damage to the Aura Portal. For theft claims, a Police Report Number from a local law enforcement agency is mandatory.

Adjuster Assignment: Within 48 business hours, an Aura Field Adjuster (Level II or higher) will be assigned. For claims under $5,000, we may offer "Virtual Adjustment" via a live video call.

Settlement: Once approved, funds are disbursed via ACH transfer within 3–5 business days or via a physical check sent via USPS Priority Mail.
