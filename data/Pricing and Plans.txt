Aura Assurance utilizes a tiered pricing model based on the "Aura Risk Score," a proprietary metric calculated using regional weather data, local crime rates, and the age of the structure’s roof. Pricing is updated annually on April 1.

Standard Tier Structures:

Essential Tier: Starting at $85/month. Includes a $2,500 deductible. Best for new homeowners with modern construction (post-2015).

Premier Tier: Starting at $145/month. Includes a $1,000 deductible and "Full Replacement Cost" coverage for contents.

Elite Tier: Starting at $210/month. Includes a $500 deductible, identity theft protection, and equipment breakdown coverage for HVAC systems.

Available Discounts:

Multi-Policy Bundle: Customers who combine Auto and Home insurance receive a 12.5% discount on both premiums.

Smart Home Credit: A 5% reduction is applied for homes equipped with monitored water-leak sensors and smoke detectors (Proof of monitoring contract required).

Loyalty Reward: A 2% discount is applied for every 3 consecutive years a customer remains with Aura Assurance, capped at a maximum of 10%.

Payments can be made monthly via ACH or annually. Annual payments receive a one-time $50 "Paperless Processing" credit.
